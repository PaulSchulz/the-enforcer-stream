\recette{Meringues}
\preptime{15 min} \baketime{120\textdegree C/250\textdegree F, 1 hr 30 min} \cake{10}

\recipe{
                  4 & Egg whites, at room temperature \\
              Pinch & Salt \\
      \unit[1]{cup} & Caster sugar \\
    \unit[1/2]{tsp} & Vanilla essence \\
}{
    \item Preheat oven to 120\textdegree C/110\textdegree C fan-forced.
    Line two baking trays with non-stick baking paper.
    
    \item Use an electric mixer to whisk egg whites and salt
    until soft peaks form. Gradually add suger, a tablespoon 
    at a time, whisking well between each addition, until 
    sugar dissolves.

    \item Once all the the suger has been added, continue to
    whisk on high for 3 mins. Whisk in vanilla essence.

    \item Use two teaspoons to spoon meringue onto
    lined trays. Reduce oven temperature to to 90\textdegree C.

    \item Bake meringues for 1h hour 30 mins. 
    Turn oven off and leave meringues in oven to cool 
    completely. Store in a airtight container for up to
    1 week.
}
